We all love to share on the internet. Delicious is a great service for social bookmarking, and I was looking for a way to just display a few of my more recent shares.

Most of the things that came up with a quick search for such a tool involved things like importing Delicious links into your blogroll. I wasn't havin it so I whipped up this simple widget to display a specified amount of your most recent delicious links.

Easy to Setup

Just place your delicious username and password in the widget and select your count. Done.

Easy to use

Just keep on bookmarking, and the delicious links widget will keep up with your most recent shares and display them wherever you decide to place this tasty widget.
