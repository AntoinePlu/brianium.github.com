'-' _yoast_wpseo_sitemap-prio: ! '-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- I have always been a fan of series. I think particularly of the "Sessions" you can find on Envato sites like NetTuts. My main goal creating a series is not just to teach others, but mainly to teach myself. It is a good way to organize goals and document your progress through something. I want to grab a good book or a couple of other resources and just set a goal to finish learning a subject in 10 days, 20 days, a month, etc... I develop in different areas. I have made applications in ASP.NET, PHP, JSP, Python, and the standard web technologies (JavaScript, CSS, HTML). I have mastered a few, but some feel like a familiarity. I want to visit and revisit, attack and conquer, really broaden my horizons. In this first series I am going to be going mostly through some screen casts and Imar Spaanjaars' book Beginning ASP.NET 4: in C# and VB with a special focus on C# as the backbone of my sources for this endeavor. I will document what I cover - even if it is old hat to me. I'll develop some examples and really dive deep into the framework.
